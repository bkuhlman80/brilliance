*Working document. v3.0. May 2026.*

## Scope and Content Types

The framework is a theory of personality and capacity. Both are read off a single Venn at each cell. There is one Venn per cell. Its regions are not all the same kind of thing. They sort into five content-types:

- **Motivations** sit at the four single-motivation regions. A motivation is one of the unit's primal directives.
- **Tendencies** sit at the four adjacent pairs. A tendency is the dispositional expression of two adjacent motivations together.
- **Signatures** sit at the two opposite pairs. A signature is the level-defining coupling of two motivations across the Venn.
- **Capacities** sit at the four triples. A capacity is a synthesis of three motivations the unit can perform.
- **Breakthroughs** sit at the center. A breakthrough is the unique synthesis that requires all four motivations.

Personality and capacity are content types occupying different regions of one diagram, not two parallel diagrams.

The 4-circle Venn formalism follows from the four cybernetic functions: R1 Effector, R2 Regulator, R3 Modeler, R4 Reviser. The regions carry different content types because of the Port-Royal comprehension and extension gradient. A region defined by more attributes has richer comprehension and smaller extension. A one-attribute region holds a single motivation. A two-attribute region holds either a tendency or a signature: an adjacent pair holds a tendency, an opposite pair holds a signature. A three-attribute region, a triple, holds a capacity. A capacity is a synthesis of three motivations the unit can perform. The four-attribute center holds the breakthrough, the unique synthesis that requires all four. The gradient is developed in `Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md`.

The framework is structural-explanatory first. Specific testable predictions are extracted in the journal-paper pipeline rather than enumerated here.

ABHOP's three lines (genetic, neuronal, symbolic) instantiate the bio-psycho-social model that medicine, psychology, and sociology have converged on across the twentieth century. Bio is the genetic line. Psycho is the neuronal line. Social is the symbolic line. This is a descriptive mapping rather than a relabeling. The bio-psycho-social framing leaves underspecified what the line-specific machinery is at each line, and ABHOP supplies that structural detail.

## What Lines Are Made Of

The following foundational commitments are positioned upstream of the inheritance-lines architecture. They determine what each line does, what counts as personality at each line, and where moral status applies. The cybernetics document (`Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md`) grounds the cross-line cybernetic claim in Conant-Ashby and the cybernetic-inheritance tradition.

### Each Line Ratchets a Different Kind of Goo

The cross-line architecture has the same cybernetic decomposition (R1 Effector, R2 Regulator, R3 Modeler, R4 Reviser) at every line, instantiated in line-specific machinery. The line-specific machinery differs because each line is a different kind of goo-ratchet. Same architectural function, different goo being ratcheted.

The three kinds of goo:

- **Genetic line ratchets Boltzmann goo.** Life maintains itself far from physical equilibrium. Cells, organisms, and lineages are constellations that ratchet thermodynamic disorder by importing low-entropy energy and exporting high-entropy waste. Selection preserves the constellations that ratchet best.
- **Neuronal line ratchets phenomenal goo.** Sensorimotor reentrant signaling produces affective and motivational structure. Associative learning shapes this raw phenomenal goo into the motivational architecture that coordinates behavior. These are the trait-level dispositional structures empirically documented from vertebrates and up.
- **Symbolic line ratchets Shannon goo.** Symbols are constraints on signal-space. Lexicons, grammars, conventions, and pragmatic norms each reduce the space of legitimate messages by large factors. Cultural transmission preserves the constraints that work for coordinating groups. Empires ratchet the random message-space into shared symbolic systems.

Each line does the same Darwin-level goo reduction (selection-mediated pattern preservation) at the line level, but on a different kind of goo at the unit level. This explains why the lines are genuinely different despite sharing cybernetic architecture. Same cybernetic structure, different goo, different mechanisms, different timescales.

*Epistemic status: the genetic line's Boltzmann-goo claim follows from Deacon's Shannon-Boltzmann-Darwin framework (2007a, 2007b, 2012). The neuronal line's phenomenal-goo claim is supported by active inference (Friston) at the formal-mechanism level and by the behavioral-syndrome literature (Sih, Bell & Johnson 2004; Koolhaas) at the empirical-trait level. The symbolic claim is a framework-native application of the same principle.*

### R4 as goo-ratchet, R1 as Darwin-selected outcome

The R1-R2-R3-R4 cybernetic decomposition is not just four cybernetic functions held alongside each other. It is the variation-selection cycle made architectural:

- **R4 is variation, the goo-ratchet.** The active work that keeps the line far from equilibrium.
- **R3 is modeling.** Internal representation of what the ratchet produces.
- **R2 is stabilization.** Regulated control against stored references.
- **R1 is the selection-outcome.** What propagates when the ratchet meets Darwin.

Each line runs the same four-part cycle on its goo. Boltzmann thermodynamic chaos becomes variation through self-production, modeling through interiority, regulation through coordination, and selection of fitness. Same cycle, four times, four kinds of goo.

This is why R4 motivations introduce at the end of the prior line and carry as R4 of the new line. They are the goo the new line selects on. This is also why R1 and R4 sit at opposite ends of the variation-selection cycle. R4 generates variation. R1 is the selection-outcome. They are the two ends of the cycle.

The R4 motivation of each line names the goo each line ratchets. The R1 motivation names what Darwin selects on it:

| Line | R4 goo-ratchet | Darwin-on-goo | R1 outcome |
|--------|--------------------|---------------------|------------|
| Genetic | Self-Production (Boltzmann) | thermodynamic selection | Fitness |
| Neuronal | Excitability (FEP) | prediction-error selection | Boldness |
| Symbolic | Autonomy (Shannon) | exchange-based selection | Reciprocity |

The mapping at genetic is cleanest. Self-Production directly names Deacon's teleodynamic goo-ratchet work. The mappings at neuronal and symbolic require reading Excitability as FEP-flavored active engagement and Autonomy as self-revising symbolic constraint. These require interpretation, but they are not strained. Framework concepts often unify multiple aspects of a phenomenon.

The R2 and R3 positions also gain principled architectural roles under this reading. They are the stable structures that emerge between variation and selection: stored models (R3) and stabilized regulation (R2). The full architecture is variation, then modeling, then stabilization, then selected outcome, running on each line's goo.

### Each Line Has Its Own R0

Deacon's pre-life dynamics (homeodynamic, morphodynamic, teleodynamic processes) are the R0 of the genetic line, the groundwork that prepared chemistry for life. This pattern generalizes. Each line has its own R0, a pre-line groundwork that did the goo-ratcheting work that prepared the line for emergence. Each line's R0 is the prior line's mature output, which closes the loop.

- **R0 of genetic:** Deacon's homeodynamic, morphodynamic, and teleodynamic processes. Thermodynamic ratchets that prepared chemistry for life.
- **R0 of neuronal:** pre-neural sensory and behavioral coordination in early multicellular organisms. Phenomenal ratchets that prepared bodies for brains.
- **R0 of symbolic:** pre-symbolic animal signaling, indexical and iconic reference. Shannon ratchets that prepared cognition for symbols. Deacon's *Symbolic Species* (1997) details this transition.

### The Structural Mirror and the Domain Shift

Two patterns run together at every line.

**Pattern 1: structural mirror.** Each line repeats the same cybernetic decomposition (R1 Effector, R2 Regulator, R3 Modeler, R4 Reviser). The cybernetic structure is the same at every line because it is the minimum cybernetic decomposition.

**Pattern 2: domain shift.** Each line's primary domain of action is the goo of the line one level back. The unit at each level acts on the prior line's goo, even while it is built from that goo. Genetic units act on chemistry. Neuronal units act on biology, navigating ecological niches and finding food. Symbolic units (bands, cities, empires) act on cognition, shaping member minds via stories, norms, codes, and institutions.

The two patterns run together because the new line emerges precisely when the prior line's goo becomes complex enough that a specialized control layer is worth its overhead. Neuronal systems emerged when bodies became complex enough that distributed neural coordination paid off relative to its metabolic cost. Symbolic systems emerged when groups became complex enough that culture-based coordination outpaced direct social learning.

The framework's flora-and-fauna picture encodes this pattern. The body is genetic-line flora. Once the brain emerges, the body is what the brain rides on and operates on. Cognition is neuronal-line fauna. Once symbolic systems emerge, personality is symbolic-line flora. Each line's fauna becomes the next line's flora. Two senses of "built on the prior line" run in parallel. One is genealogical, the bridge-entity pattern, parent-of-next-line. One is functional, each line acts on the prior goo. Both are real, and they are separable. See 'Flora and Fauna Across Lines' below.

### Clade and Symbiosis Vocabulary

Each cell name is shorthand for a clade, a category of units sharing the cell's architectural signature. Eukaryote names the clade of organized-interior cells. City names the clade of city-states, urban-centered kingdoms, and other polities organized around an urban administrative core. Empire names the clade of territorial, maritime, and ideological empires. The full convention is set out in 'Cell Names as Clade Shorthand' below.

Coevolution within a line operates through two modes. Symbiosis is the merger mode, where formerly more-independent parts commit as components of a new higher-level unit. Elaboration is the non-merger mode, where a unit develops continuously without committing separable parts. R3 and R4 transitions are symbiosis-prone at every line. R1 and R2 transitions are elaboration. The line determines the mechanism. The genetic line uses free-living merger (endosymbiosis, multicellularity). The symbolic line uses polity merger (city formation, empire formation). The neuronal line uses neural-lineage stacking (agranular cortex modeling subcortical control structures at Mammals, granular cortex modeling agranular at Primates). The full account is in 'Symbiosis and Elaboration' below.

### Disease, Disorder, and Line of Failure

Failures of tendencies and capacities sort by the line at which they emerged. Genetic-line failure is disease: cells, tissues, and organs that no longer maintain homeostasis. Neuronal-line failure is disorder: dispositional constellations and capacities that no longer regulate the organism's life against contextual demands. Symbolic-line failure is disorganization: cultural, institutional, and normative structures that no longer regulate the constituent humans' collective life against contextual demands. Durkheim's anomie is the founding sociological description. Social disorganization (Shaw and McKay 1942) is the modern technical term, used across criminology, urban sociology, and family sociology. Three lines of failure, three vocabularies, three intervention modalities.

An empire-human can develop either kind. Cancer is a genetic-line failure in the same person who can also develop a personality disorder, a neuronal-line failure. Both are real failures of the unit. They live at different lines. Civilizational collapse and institutional breakdown are the symbolic-line examples of disorganization.

Failures are also modeled at higher lines than the one they live in. Genetic-line failures are modeled in the neuronal line via interoception, the organism's internal sensing of cellular and tissue state. They are modeled in the symbolic line via medical knowledge, the cultural apparatus by which physicians categorize and intervene on disease. Neuronal-line failures are modeled in the symbolic line via psychological and psychiatric frameworks, the cultural apparatus by which clinicians categorize and intervene on disorder. The line of the modeling is not the line of the failure.

The personality disorders panel sits at Empire-Human in the diagram because the industrial era is when these neuronal-line failures became serious academic objects. Freud and the early psychoanalytic tradition framed neuroses as pragmatic failures, and that framing shaped how the disorders were measured and described. The panel records the industrial-era symbolic-line model of what neuronal-line failures look like. The failures themselves live in the brain of the affected individuals, not in the symbolic line that describes them.

Why personality disorders resist medication is then explained. It is not because the failures are non-biological. It is because they are constellational rather than disease-like. The brain is not broken the way a diseased cell is broken. It is configured in a way that misfits contextual demands. Therapy works because it shifts constellation through experience-driven learning, which is the neuronal line's native modification modality. Medication targets cellular and molecular machinery, the machinery of disease rather than the machinery of disorder.

### Where Personality Lives, Where Suffering Lives

Personality and felt suffering live in different goo. Human personality disorder spans both. The failure of cultural participation is the disorder itself. The felt suffering of the disordered person is the phenomenal-goo consequence. The cultural-goo failure is real, but the phenomenal-goo felt-component is what makes it morally pressing rather than just descriptively interesting.

A superorganism is a meme-complex operating as symbolic-line fauna. It can carry personality-level dysfunction without phenomenal-goo co-location. The empire-fauna does not carry phenomenal goo. The constituent-human-flora does. When the cybernetic structure fails, the humans suffer, not the superorganism. Moral status tracks the flora at the symbolic line, because the flora is where the phenomenal goo lives.

The transition from pre-vertebrate to vertebrate-and-up may be where suffering proper becomes possible. Before motivational architecture exists at the unit level, there is no agent in the relevant sense to suffer the breakdown. Once the architecture exists, the agent can break down toward equilibrium and feel the breakdown. So suffering may be specifically post-vertebrate, with bilaterians having affective valence but not the agent-level felt structure. The moral implications are significant for invertebrate welfare debates.

*Epistemic status: the two-goo distinction is a framework theoretical commitment. The specific claim that suffering proper begins post-vertebrate is held with lower confidence and is flagged as exposed.*

### The Goo Test for a 5th Line

A new line requires a new modality of pattern transmission that can carry inheritance. Genetic, neuronal, and symbolic were each a new way of moving information across time. A 5th line, if it comes, would be identifiable by the same criterion: selection-mediated agent-to-agent inheritance, a new goo of pattern propagation. The framework does not claim such a line is forming.

The test rules out most large-scale innovation. Substantial structural complexity often appears within a line without crossing the line threshold. Coral reefs are massive elaborations at the eumetazoan level. Corporations and modern markets are massive elaborations at the symbolic line. Neither introduces a new inheritance modality. The full account is in 'Elaboration at Scale' below. Most innovation is elaboration. Lines are rare.

### Inheritance lines

Each line is a distinct inheritance modality, a way of moving information across time that wasn't available before:

- **Genetic** stores information in nucleic acid, transmits via reproduction, modifies through mutation and selection. It ratchets Boltzmann goo.
- **Neuronal** stores information in neural patterns, transmits via behavior and learning, modifies through experience. It ratchets phenomenal goo (affective and motivational structure).
- **Symbolic** stores information in network-defined signs, transmits via teaching and copying, modifies through cultural innovation. It ratchets Shannon goo.

### Row functions

Row function definitions live in `Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md`. The architectural summary:

- **R1 Effector** executes the line's native verb (reproduce in genetic, act in neuronal, exchange in symbolic).
- **R2 Regulator** stabilizes behavior against stored references.
- **R3 Modeler** maintains internal representation that organizes inputs.
- **R4 Reviser** revises the references the regulator stabilizes against.

The four functions emerge in order at every line because each depends on the prior. There is no Regulator without an Effector to regulate. There is no Modeler without something worth representing. There is no Reviser without a model worth updating.

### Adjacent pairs, triples, and opposite pairs

Adjacent pairs instantiate four loop-functions. They carry tendencies, the dispositional pair-expressions of two adjacent motivations:

- **Controller** (R1×R2): closed-loop control, action plus stabilization.
- **Anticipator** (R2×R3): model-informed regulation.
- **Adapter** (R3×R4): self-revising model.
- **Explorer** (R4×R1): exploratory action.

"Tendency" is a permissive umbrella term. At genetic-line cells the adjacent pairs hold genetic-line dispositional capabilities such as Metabolic Type and Taxis Type. At neuronal and symbolic cells the adjacent pairs hold personality traits such as Coping Style and Conscientiousness. The umbrella term spans the lines. The specific named constructs differ by cell and by the literature anchoring them.

The adjacent-pair labels follow a naming convention by line. The genetic-line adjacent pairs tend toward Type suffixes. The neuronal-line adjacent pairs tend toward Style suffixes. The symbolic-line adjacent pairs tend toward -ness or related nominal suffixes. The convention is descriptive of the empirical literatures the constructs anchor in, not prescriptive.

Triples (one motivation absent) instantiate four synthesis-functions. They carry capacities, syntheses of three motivations the unit can perform:

- **Reactor** (absent R3): action and stabilization and revision without internal model. Model-free regulated action.
- **Conservator** (absent R4): action and stabilization and representation without revision. The mature loop running without meta-update.
- **Planner** (absent R1): regulation and representation and revision without execution. Strategic deliberation.
- **Pursuer** (absent R2): action and representation and revision without stabilization. Goal-pursuit without rule-enforced inhibition.

The two opposite pairs carry signatures, the level-defining couplings of two motivations across the Venn. The R1×R3 Connector hosts Lineage, Host, Temperament, Self-Model, Self-Reference, and Self-Evaluation across the cells. The R2×R4 Operator hosts Organism, Reflex Repertoire, Behavioral Syndrome, Personality, Self-Construal, and Identity. Each signature persists for two consecutive cells, the cell where it is introduced plus the next. It then shifts when one of its two contributing motivations is eclipsed. See `Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md`.

The 4-circle Venn has 15 regions: 4 single-motivation regions (motivations), 4 adjacent pairs (tendencies), 2 opposite pairs (signatures), 4 triples (capacities), and 1 center (the breakthrough).

### The rolling window

Each cell holds 4 foreground motivations. Across cells, the motivations slide. The cycle is regular. Each new cell introduces one motivation at one slot, eclipsing the motivation that held that slot at the cell below. The introduction order is R2 then R3 then R4 then R1, repeating across the matrix.

Across the 12 cells from Protocell through Empires, the cycle runs three full rotations. The R1 swap is a line boundary (Fitness to Boldness, Boldness to Reciprocity). At every other swap the line persists.

When a motivation drops out of the foreground window, it does not disappear. Interiority still operates in primates. Coordination still operates in vertebrates. Fitness still operates in everything alive. The claim is more precise. Older motivations get backgrounded into infrastructure that newer attractors run on. They become difficult for observers to discriminate against the signal of more recent attractors. The framework is measurement-theoretic, not architectural-deletion.

### The Breakthrough

The center of each Venn is the breakthrough. It is the synthetic capability that emerges when all four foreground motivations operate together. It is not a fifth motivation. It is what the four jointly produce. Examples are Natural Selection at Protocell, Steering at Bilaterian, Speaking at Band-Human, Codes at City-Human, and Stories at Empire-Human. The term follows Bennett, whose neuronal-line breakthroughs (Steering, Reinforcing, Simulating, Mentalizing) the framework already uses. Per-cell breakthroughs live in the Per-Cell Treatments below.

### Unit-level subjectivity

The unified Venn at each cell applies to a specific unit.

- **Genetic-line cells.** The unit is the cell or organism: Protocell-as-unit, Prokaryote-as-unit, Eukaryote-as-unit, Eumetazoa-as-unit.
- **Neuronal-line cells.** The unit is the brain-bearing organism: Bilaterian-as-unit, Vertebrate-as-unit, Mammal-as-unit, Primate-as-unit.
- **Symbolic-line cells.** The unit is the constituent human parameterized by collective context: Band-Human, Settlement-Human, City-Human, Empire-Human. The collective remains the group. The Venn at the symbolic cell tracks the constituent human as unit. See "Flora and Fauna Across Lines" below for how the collective relates to the human as meme-fauna to personality-flora.

The systematic-analogy rule lets motivations transfer up the stack by structural analogy, not loose metaphor. At symbolic cells the motivations are held by the constituent human. What shifts across cells is which primaries are foreground given collective context. A Band-Human's Reciprocity is the band-context-conditioned attractor in the same organism that, in a different context, would foreground different motivations.

### Adjacent-pair, triple, and opposite-pair persistence

Each level transition introduces 6 new labels: 2 new adjacent pairs, 3 new triples, and 1 new signature. These are the regions that include the just-introduced motivation. It persists 4 labels from the cell below: 2 adjacent pairs, 1 triple, and 1 signature, the regions that do not include the new motivation. Persistence is exact. Any cell whose count breaks indicates a hole in the label library.

Protocell is the founding cell and has no cell below to persist from. It carries a Connector signature, Lineage, but its Operator is empty, since an Operator signature requires a prior introduction. Every cell from Prokaryote onward carries both a Connector and an Operator signature.

A vocabulary shift runs at the genetic-to-neuronal boundary. Genetic-line labels are drawn from cell biology and developmental biology. Neuronal-line labels are drawn from behavioral ecology and personality psychology. The constellations that bridge the boundary carry different labels on either side. This is a deliberate vocabulary shift, not a persistence failure. The disciplines that built the vocabularies anchored at different cells.

## Flora and Fauna Across Lines

Each line contains two ecological roles. Flora are sessile, persistent, and environment-shaping. They are slow on the line's timescale. They generate the environment that fauna consume. Fauna are motile and predatory. They are fast on the line's timescale. They are behaviorally flexible relative to the flora they inhabit.

Humans are to culture as plants are to animals: we are flora. The relationship is loopy, not simple. Animals forage on plants, and plants coevolve with animals, which is to say plants shape animals in turn. Memes forage on human brains, and personalities coevolve with culture, which is to say personalities shape culture in turn.

The roles populate every line:

| Line | Flora | Fauna |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Genetic | Plants and their predecessors | Animals |
| Neuronal | Bodies as organism | Brains and nervous systems |
| Symbolic | Personalities | Memes and meme-complexes |

Each line's fauna becomes the next line's flora. Animals do not replace plants. Brains do not replace bodies. Memes do not replace personalities. The relationship is coevolutionary. The new fauna develops alongside what becomes its new flora, and the two remain interdependent.

The symbolic line's flora is the constituent human's personality structure. This is the trait-level structure that personality psychology measures. The symbolic line's fauna is the full spectrum of memes, from small ideas to large meme-complexes. Superorganisms are large meme-complexes operating as fauna. A band as a whole, a settlement as a whole, a city as a whole, and an empire as a whole are all superorganisms. They are not parallel units of personality to the constituent human. They are the fauna that inhabits the human-flora personality.

A single human participates in three lines at once at three different ecological positions. The human body is genetic-line flora. The human brain is neuronal-line fauna. The human personality is symbolic-line flora. The three roles coexist in one organism.

Flora are not passive in any line. Plants manipulate pollinators, shape soil chemistry, and structure ecosystems on long timescales. Bodies grow brains, regulate metabolic resources, and determine the niches brains operate in. Personalities generate the symbolic content memes feed on, every utterance and artifact and child raised. The flora's mode of action runs longer and broader than the fauna's. The fauna's mode of action runs faster and more locally focal than the flora's. Both are active.

## Coevolution Across Line Transitions

Each line emergence is a coevolutionary event. The new fauna develops alongside what becomes its new flora. It does not replace the prior line. It enters into stable interdependence with the prior line's mature output. The pattern recurs across the framework's structural history:

- Cambrian explosion (~540 MYA): vertebrate brains coevolve with the diversification of bilaterian body plans.
- Land transition (~400 MYA): land arthropods coevolve with land plants.
- Tetrapod radiation (~360 MYA): land tetrapods coevolve with forests.
- Angiosperm radiation (~140 MYA): social insects coevolve with flowering plants.
- Mesozoic transition (~200 to 66 MYA): mammals coevolve with dinosaurs.
- Cenozoic transition (~66 MYA onward): birds and primates coevolve with post-extinction forest niches.
- Symbolic line emergence (~100 KYA onward): human personality coevolves with human culture.

The bridge-entity pattern (R4 of column N parents R1 of column N+1) traces the genealogical thread through these events. Coevolution is the ecological story running alongside the genealogical one. The two are not in tension. The bridge identifies which entity opens the new line. Coevolution describes how the line fills in once opened.

*Epistemic status: the deep-time coevolutionary events are well-documented in evolutionary biology and paleontology. The reading of symbolic-line emergence as a coevolution of human personality with human culture is a framework extension of the same pattern.*

## Symbiosis and Elaboration

Coevolution operates through two modes. Symbiosis is the merger mode. A new higher-level structure forms by committing previously-more-independent parts as its components. Elaboration is the non-merger mode. A unit develops continuously without committing previously separable parts.

R3 and R4 transitions are symbiosis-prone at every line. R1 and R2 transitions are elaboration. The line determines what symbiosis looks like.

- **Genetic R3 endosymbiosis:** free-living prokaryotes commit as mitochondria, and as chloroplasts in plants, inside a host cell.
- **Genetic R4 multicellularity:** free-living eukaryotic cells commit as differentiated tissues inside a multicellular body.
- **Neuronal R3 cortical stacking:** mammals introduce agranular cortex as a new neural lineage layered over the subcortical control structures (basal ganglia, limbic system). The agranular cortex's function is to model what the subcortical structures do. The two lineages persist in obligate functional interdependence.
- **Neuronal R4 cortical stacking:** primates introduce granular cortex as a meta-modeling layer over the agranular cortex. The same architectural signature, applied one level up.
- **Symbolic R3 city formation:** settlements commit as components of a city, and trade networks commit as the connective tissue that lets the city aggregate goods, people, and information beyond what any single settlement can produce.
- **Symbolic R4 empire formation:** cities and their hinterlands commit as administrative provinces of an empire. Empires often add colonies that extend the empire beyond contiguous territory.

R1 and R2 transitions are elaboration in every line. No merger of previously separable parts is required. The unit develops continuously from existing structure.

- **Genetic R1 elaboration:** protocells emerge from teleodynamic ratchets through continuous chemical development.
- **Genetic R2 elaboration:** prokaryotes emerge from protocell logic through stored regulation.
- **Neuronal R1 elaboration:** bilaterian brains emerge from genetic-R4 neural nets through elaboration of a control architecture for reflexes (steering and affect).
- **Neuronal R2 elaboration:** vertebrates elaborate the bilaterian control architecture with reinforcement learning (basal ganglia plus operant conditioning).
- **Symbolic R1 elaboration:** bands emerge from primate troop dynamics through language-mediated extension.
- **Symbolic R2 elaboration:** settlements emerge from band practices through sedentary agriculture.

The neuronal version of symbiosis runs through neural-lineage stacking because the brain is body-internal. There is no free-living state from which neural lineages could merge. What is available is the structural pattern where a newer neural lineage develops the capacity to model an older one it is built on top of. Bennett's reinterpretation of MacLean's triune brain (*A Brief History of Intelligence*, 2023) supplies the mechanism. MacLean's stacking intuition was partly right; what he got wrong was the specific lineage details, not the layered architecture.

*Epistemic status: the R3-and-R4 symbiosis pattern holds across all three lines with two confirmed cases at each row in the genetic and symbolic lines. The neuronal-line version, instantiated through cortical-lineage stacking, is well-documented at the structural-anatomical level. The framework's claim that the neuronal stacking pattern carries the same architectural signature as endosymbiosis and polity merger is a framework theoretical commitment.*

## Cell Names as Clade Shorthand

Each cell name is shorthand for a clade. A clade is a category of units sharing the cell's architectural signature. The cell name is not a single named entity. The cell name picks out the representative form. The clade contains internal diversity.

The convention applies across lines:

- **Eukaryote** names the clade containing protists, fungi, plants, and animals. All share the organized-interior architecture with membrane-bounded organelles.
- **Eumetazoa** names the clade containing all multicellular animals with differentiated tissues.
- **Bilaterian** names the clade containing all bilaterally symmetric animals with centralized nervous systems.
- **Mammal** names the clade containing all mammals, with their many brain region elaborations.
- **City** names the clade containing classic city-states, urban-centered kingdoms, and other forms of polity organized around an urban administrative core. The city-clade contains both the city-as-polity (Sumerian Uruk, Greek polis, Italian Renaissance city-states) and the larger state-with-central-city forms (Pharaonic Egypt, Han China, the Achaemenid satrapies). Cities emerge from the symbiosis of settlements and trade networks. Settlements supply the population and agricultural-surplus base. Trade networks supply the connectivity that lets the city aggregate goods, people, and information beyond what any single settlement can produce. The city is the merger product.
- **Empire** names the clade containing territorial empires, maritime empires, and ideological empires. All share the move of binding multiple cities, and often colonies, under a universalizing framework.

Each clade is built from instances of the prior clade as constituent components. Eukaryotes contain prokaryote-derived organelles. Eumetazoa contain eukaryotic cells. Cities contain settlements as their agricultural and demographic base, with trade-network connectivity threading through them. Empires contain cities and their hinterlands. The constituent is the clade's structural innovation. The clade name picks out the level of organization built from that constituent.

## Per-Cell Treatments

The twelve filled cells of the matrix each get one section below. Each section opens with structural commentary that covers the cell's breakthrough, virtuous cycle, and any cell-specific notes about opposite-pair signatures or bridge-entity status. One unified Venn table follows.

### Genetic Line

The genetic line ratchets Boltzmann goo through nucleic-acid inheritance. Its R0 is Deacon's homeodynamic, morphodynamic, and teleodynamic processes, the pre-biotic ratchets that prepared chemistry for life. The genetic line's primaries at the bottom of the stack (Dissipation, Self-Organization, Self-Production) are this R0 groundwork carried forward as the founding cell's working motivations. Fitness, the line's R1 motivation, is what Darwin selects when ratchets exist.

Genetic-line cells have no documented personality-trait structure. Trait-level individual differences in dispositional response, the content personality psychology studies, are not documented at any cellular or multicellular level below the brain-bearing organism. The tendencies at the adjacent pairs of a genetic-line Venn are dispositional capabilities of the cell-as-unit, not personality traits. The signatures at the opposite pairs are cell-level couplings of the same kind.

#### Protocell Stack

The founding form of the genetic line. The line-defining move is the move from chemistry to inheritance. The defining mechanism is autocatalytic ratcheting: RNA sequences that catalyze their own replication, where information and function are the same molecule. Once ratchets exist, *Fitness* operates as an attractor immediately. Any sequence that replicates more reliably outcompetes any sequence that doesn't. Selection pressure on replication fidelity is what the rest of biology runs on.

The reinforcing cycle requires three conditions to hold jointly: a medium (water), a container (lipid vesicle), and periodic energy gradients (tidal, thermal, redox cycles). When all three hold, autopoiesis closes the loop. The breakthrough is Natural Selection itself, named at the level of what the Protocell-as-unit can do that nothing below can: ratchet pattern persistence through differential survival.

The three R0 groundwork processes carry as foreground motivations at Protocell. Dissipation is self-maintenance against thermodynamic decay. Self-Organization is form-building dynamics. Self-Production is the work-doing dynamics that prepared chemistry for ends-directed behavior. Each relabels one of Deacon's R0 processes: Dissipation the homeodynamic, Self-Organization the morphodynamic, Self-Production the teleodynamic.

The R1×R3 Connector introduces Lineage, the framework's first signature. Lineage names the persistent replicating-form chain, the continuity of the replicating form across copies. The R2×R4 Operator is empty at Protocell, since an Operator signature requires a prior cell to carry one from.

The R4×R1 Explorer carries Acquisition Type, which spans the genetic line's exploratory uptake of exogenous functional content: horizontal gene transfer, plasmid uptake, transformation, conjugation, transduction, endosymbiosis, and viral integration. The label names the broad phenomenon across the Protocell-Prokaryote-Eukaryote span rather than any one prokaryote-specific mechanism.

#### Prokaryote Stack

#### Eukaryote Stack

Eukaryotes introduce organized interiority in the genetic register: a cell with a defended interior containing organelles that maintain their own operating conditions. The nucleus protects DNA. The mitochondrion runs an electrochemical gradient at conditions hostile to the rest of the cell. The defining mechanism is endosymbiosis: capturing another organism and operating it as a component. *Interiority* operates as the attractor in the literal sense. Maintaining the interior against the exterior is what staying alive consists of.

The bioenergetic transition through mitochondria is Nick Lane's territory. The eukaryotic cell is the bioenergetic platform that scaling everything else up depends on. The breakthrough is Scale & Size. Eukaryotes are large compared to prokaryotes and complex compared to prokaryotes. The difference traces to Energy-Production Type at the SW Adapter position.

The reinforcing cycle runs through mating and meiosis (sexual recombination plus the cell-division machinery that supports it), memory beyond moment-to-moment adaptation, and a hierarchical behavioral repertoire (nested programs invokable under different conditions, all running inside one cell). The R3 swap at this cell replaces Self-Organization with Interiority. Fitness, Coordination, and Self-Production persist.

The R1×R3 Connector introduces Host, the signature that names the chimeric unit constituted by its incorporated symbiont, canonically the mitochondrion. The R2×R4 Operator carries Organism forward from Prokaryote.

### Neuronal Line

The neuronal line ratchets phenomenal goo: affective and motivational structure produced by sensorimotor reentrant signaling and shaped by associative learning. Its R0 is the pre-neural sensory and behavioral coordination that emerged in early multicellular organisms.

Trait-level individual differences in dispositional response are documented from Vertebrates up, in behavioral-syndrome research in behavioral ecology and in Koolhaas coping-style architecture. At those cells the adjacent pairs carry personality traits and the opposite pairs carry signatures.

#### Bilaterian Stack

The founding form of the neuronal line. The line-defining move is from diffuse nerve nets to centralized brains with monoamine signaling. Dopamine and serotonin emerge as the monoamines of valence and arousal. The defining mechanism is steering: head-first directional movement that requires a brain to process forward sensory input and command body-wide muscular response. *Boldness* operates as the attractor because steering only pays off if the organism actually moves into novel space. A steering animal that doesn't venture forward gains nothing from the architecture.

The reinforcing cycle runs through steering plus affect. Affect is valence and arousal, the basic good-or-bad evaluation of states. Steering plus affect produces associative learning, which makes future steering more rewarding, which selects for finer steering and richer affect. The breakthrough is Steering, the new capability that defines the cell.

The R1 swap at Bilaterians replaces Fitness with Boldness, marking the genetic-to-neuronal line boundary. Coordination, Interiority, and Excitability persist as foreground motivations from Eumetazoa. The R1×R3 Connector introduces Temperament, the founding form of the Connector axis (see `Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md`). The R2×R4 Operator carries Reflex Repertoire forward from Eumetazoa, since Coordination and Excitability both persist.

#### Vertebrate Stack

Vertebrates introduce stored regulation in the neuronal register: behavior governed by adjustable rules that don't require rewriting wiring. The defining mechanism is operant conditioning, action-outcome contingencies reshaping future action selection. *Mastery* operates as the attractor because the organism is now in the business of getting better at things. Skill rather than just response.

The reinforcing cycle runs through the basal ganglia in actor-critic form. The actor selects actions. The critic evaluates outcomes. The reward prediction error updates the actor (Sutton & Barto). Procedural memory consolidates frequently-rewarded action sequences. Model-free reinforcement learning is the formal description of what the system is doing. This is Bennett's Reinforcing breakthrough, basal ganglia plus operant conditioning, and it is the breakthrough at this cell.

The vertebrate cell is where trait-level individual differences in dispositional response first become a documented empirical structure. The Behavioral Syndrome construct sits at the R2×R4 Operator coupling Mastery with Excitability. Proactive versus reactive coping styles sort on the SNS-HPA ratio with dopamine as the coupling neuromodulator (Koolhaas's two-tier architecture). Sih, Bell & Johnson (2004) supplies the cross-lineage empirical anchor in behavioral ecology, suites of correlated behaviors across contexts documented across fish, birds, and mammals. See `Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md` for the per-construct treatment.

The adjacent pairs at Vertebrate carry the first documented personality tendencies, the fish-and-up vertebrate personality structure measured in behavioral ecology (Koolhaas coping styles, Réale's pace-of-life dimensions). The R2×R4 Operator introduces Behavioral Syndrome. The R1×R3 Connector carries Temperament forward from Bilaterian.

The R2×R3 Anticipator carries Development Rate. Development Rate is the model-informed-regulation construct at this cell. It anchors on pace-of-life syndrome and life-history strategy. The organism regulates faster and slower developmental trajectories against a model of expected life-history pace.

The Planner triple carries Gut Sense. Gut Sense covers implicit forecasting, the allostatic regulation analogous to the Bilaterian Planner. It also covers the vertebrate enteric nervous system. The two together name forecasting that runs without explicit deliberation.

The R2 swap replaces Coordination with Mastery. Boldness, Interiority, and Excitability persist.

#### Mammal Stack

Mammals introduce organized interiority in the neuronal register: a brain with multiple co-occurring functional architectures running in parallel (agranular PFC, limbic system, hippocampus). The defining mechanism is active inference: perception and action as joint minimization of prediction error against a generative model (Friston). The brain models future states using working memory and proto-episodic memory rather than only responding to current input.

*Affiliation* operates as the attractor because the same machinery enables the attachment behavior system (Bowlby). The parent infers the offspring's needs. The offspring forms an internal working model of the parent. Bonded relationships become a regulated motivation rather than just a response.

The reinforcing cycle runs through simulation. The brain selects action to achieve a modeled future state. The resulting trajectories feed back into the generative model. The breakthrough is Simulating. Simulating is where active inference lives in the framework. Active inference (Friston) and Bennett's Simulating breakthrough are the same construct at the four-way intersection. The episodic-memory system is its hardware.

The R2×R3 Anticipator carries Experiencing Style. Experiencing Style anchors on Kahneman's experiencing self (Thinking, Fast and Slow, 2011). Attachment-style content (Bowlby, Ainsworth) sits here. Attachment style is an experiencing-style construct, since the experiencing self is fundamentally relational under mastery-grounded regulation.

The R3×R4 Adapter carries Remembering Style. Remembering Style anchors on Kahneman's remembering self. The retrospective-construction architecture is its hardware. Remembering Style parallels Bowlby's internal-working-model construct and Freud's ego construct.

The triples at Mammal carry the Stanovich tri-process constructs. The Conservator carries Autonomous Cognition, Stanovich Type 1, the autonomous mind running without meta-update. The Planner carries Reflective Cognition, Stanovich Type 3, the reflective mind that regulates and represents and revises without execution. The Pursuer carries Algorithmic Cognition, Stanovich Type 2, the algorithmic mind that computes without rule-enforced inhibition.

The R3 swap replaces Interiority with Affiliation. Boldness, Mastery, and Excitability persist. The R1×R3 Connector introduces Self-Model. Self-Model is Bennett's term for the mammalian self-representation, the modeled agent that pairs with Simulating at the breakthrough position. The R2×R4 Operator carries Behavioral Syndrome forward from Vertebrate (see `Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md`).

#### Primate Stack

The granular prefrontal cortex made mentalization possible: modeling other minds as having intentions distinct from one's own. Mentalization makes *Autonomy* operate as a stable attractor. An agent that can model what others want is also an agent that can pursue what *it* wants against group pressure.

The reinforcing cycle runs through politicking. Mentalization enables alliance-building, which raises social complexity, which selects for deeper mentalization. Metabolic cost is paid by a high-energy fruit diet, which confines the lineage to arboreal niches and limits troop mobility. The bet trades ecological range for cognitive depth. Primates have been losing territory steadily for millions of years to keep paying for it. This is Bennett's Mentalizing breakthrough, modeling other minds and modeling one's own modeling, and it is the breakthrough at this cell.

The R3×R4 Adapter carries Agreeableness. Agreeableness is the trait debut. It arises at the Affiliation × Autonomy intersection. Mentalization is the mechanism that enables it. ### Symbolic Line

The symbolic line ratchets Shannon goo. Symbols are constraints on signal-space. Lexicons, grammars, conventions, and pragmatic norms each reduce the space of legitimate messages by large factors. Cultural transmission preserves the constraints that work for coordinating groups. Its R0 is pre-symbolic animal signaling (indexical and iconic reference), detailed in Deacon's *Symbolic Species* (1997).

The unified Venn at each symbolic-line cell takes the constituent human as its unit, parameterized by collective context. The collective is the group the human runs on. A band produces Speaking. A settlement produces Norms. A city produces Codes. An empire produces Stories. The cells are named with the -Human suffix (Band-Human, Settlement-Human, City-Human, Empire-Human) to mark that the Venn tracks the constituent human, not the collective. See "Flora and Fauna Across Lines" for the relation between the human-flora and the collective-fauna.

#### Band-Human Stack

The founding form of the symbolic line. The line-defining move is from primate-style alliance politics to language-mediated social ledgers. The defining mechanism is gossip, which builds the symbolic layer that makes social debt trackable across many people without direct observation. *Reciprocity* operates as the attractor because language-tracked debts make non-kin cooperation enforceable. Cheaters get talked about. Contributors get talked about. The band as a whole maintains a distributed ledger of who owes what to whom.

The reinforcing cycle runs through meat pooling (calorie-rich food shared across the band rather than monopolized), intergroup marriage (formalized cross-band reproductive alliance), and collaborative fission-fusion (groups split and rejoin without rupture because the ledger persists). This unlocks dispersal to all habitable latitudes and cumulative culture, where each generation inherits the previous generation's solutions. The breakthrough is Speaking.

The R1 swap at Bands replaces Boldness with Reciprocity, marking the neuronal-to-symbolic line boundary. Mastery, Affiliation, and Autonomy persist from Primates. Band-Human emerges by elaboration, not symbiosis: it develops from primate troop dynamics through language-mediated extension.

The R1×R2 Controller carries Standing. Standing is the trait of being skilled enough to discharge social-ledger debts. It sits at the Reciprocity × Mastery intersection.

The R3×R4 Adapter carries Agreeableness forward from Primate.

The R4×R1 Explorer carries Neuroticism. Neuroticism debuts at Band-Human. It is the trait of disturbed exploration under autonomous-reciprocal action. This is Big Five Neuroticism's structural slot.

The R1×R3 Connector introduces Self-Reference. Self-Reference anchors on Hofstadter's strange-loop pattern (I Am a Strange Loop, 2007) and the self-conscious emotions (Tracy and Robins; Lewis; Tangney). The self-conscious emotions are shame, guilt, embarrassment, and pride. The strange-loop architecture is the brain's mechanism for self-referential consciousness. The self-conscious emotions are how that self-reference manifests under symbolic-reciprocal evaluation. See `Personality, Evolution, and Cybernetics.md`.

The R2×R4 Operator carries Personality forward from Primate. The Planner triple carries Common Knowledge forward from Primate.

#### Settlement-Human Stack

The reinforcing cycle runs through surplus. Agriculture produces surplus. Surplus enables labor specialization (rulers, priests, scribes, soldiers, craftsmen). #### City-Human Stack

Cities introduce organized representation in the symbolic register. The defining mechanism is the administrative code: written rule that records obligation, ownership, and procedure in a form that outlives the people who set it down. *Representation* operates as the attractor because a city runs on standing records. Land registers, law codes, tax rolls, and calendars let coordination scale past what any group can hold in memory.

The city forms as a symbiosis of settlements and trade networks. Settlements supply the population, surplus, and agricultural base. Trade networks supply the connectivity that lets the city aggregate goods, people, and information beyond what any single settlement can produce. The city is the merger product. Historical instances include the Sumerian city-states forming as administrative consolidations of agricultural villages around urban centers along Mesopotamian trade routes, Pharaonic Egyptian urban centers along the Nile, Shang dynasty China consolidating Neolithic settlement clusters around early urban centers along trade corridors, and the Indus Valley urban centers (Mohenjo-Daro, Harappa) at the western terminus of overland Asian trade.

The merchant emerges as a new constituent-human type at this level, alongside the band-forager and the settled farmer. The merchant is a symbolic-line nomad: a human whose life-form is structured around movement along trade-network routes between cities, distinct from both the still-extant hunter-gatherer-band-nomad and the settled farmer.

The reinforcing cycle runs through records. Records enable taxation. Taxation funds specialists. Specialists produce more records. The breakthrough is Codes.

#### Empire-Human Stack

The empire forms as a symbiosis of cities and colonies committing to integration under a universalizing framework. Cities and their hinterlands supply the political-administrative base. Colonies supply the territorial extension beyond contiguous imperial territory, often resource-extractive or strategic-positional. ## Bridge-Entity Pattern

A structural feature runs across the two confirmed line transitions: the entity at Row 4 of column N parents the entity at Row 1 of column N+1.

- Eumetazoa (Row 4, genetic) parents Bilaterians (Row 1, neuronal).
- Primates (Row 4, neuronal) parents Bands (Row 1, symbolic).

The R4 motivation introduced at the parent cell carries as the R4 motivation of the child cell. Excitability introduced at Eumetazoa is R4 of Bilaterians. Autonomy introduced at Primates is R4 of Bands. The variation-selection reading (see 'Each Line Ratchets a Different Kind of Goo' above) is that R4 is the goo each line ratchets, on which the new line selects.

Sample size is 2. Two confirmed bridges is enough to name the pattern but not enough to treat the pattern as architectural law.

Row 4 entities are defined by the riskiness of the structural bet they represent. Three fates are possible and distinguishable only retroactively. The bet fails. The bet succeeds as itself but stays off-thread, a real innovation that does not open a next inheritance line, the way fungi and plants are evolutionary triumphs that did not parent further lines. The bet bridges, parenting the Row 1 founding form of a next line. Empire-Human is the framework's current Row 4 of the most-evolved column. The framework does not claim which of the three fates is in progress.

## Off-Thread Branches

The 4×4 traces one phylogenetic thread: the specific lineage from protocell through bilaterians, vertebrates, mammals, primates, into bands, settlements, cities, and empires. It is the thread that produced all four inheritance-line transitions observed so far.

Off-thread lineages (plants, fungi, slime molds, birds, bees, octopi) have their own stacks built from the same architecture, but with different motivations filling the slots above their branching point.

Bees branched at bilaterians, so their stack shares Fitness, Coordination, Interiority, Excitability, and Boldness with the human thread. Above that, bees develop motivations specific to eusocial arthropods, and the lineage stops there because the bee thread did not produce a symbolic line.

This converts an apparent limitation into a feature. Any organism's personality can be analyzed as a 4-sphere active window over its own evolutionary stack. The 4×4 is the case where the lineage runs all the way to symbols. The architecture applies to any thread of life.

Parallel evolution becomes visible without forcing convergence. Octopi branched at bilaterians and developed sophisticated cognition through a different neural architecture. Their stack would resemble the vertebrate stack at certain levels but with different motivations filling the upper slots. The framework predicts structural similarity at the levels of shared ancestry and divergence above the branching point.

### Elaboration at Scale

Substantial structural innovation often happens within a line without producing a new line. The goo test stays operative. A new line requires a new modality of pattern transmission that carries inheritance. Elaboration produces enormous complexity without crossing the line threshold.

Coral reefs are a clean example at the eumetazoan level. Reefs are massive multi-species ecosystems built around cnidarian colonies. They contain fish, invertebrates, symbiotic algae, and many other species. Their ecological productivity rivals tropical rainforests. They are not a new phylum. They do not introduce a new inheritance modality. The cnidarian biology that builds them remains within the eumetazoan-R4 logic.

Corporations, modern markets, and exchanges are the parallel case at the symbolic line. They are massive multi-institutional structures built around late-empire elaboration. They contain legal frameworks, financial instruments, supply chains, regulatory bodies, and currencies. Their structural complexity rivals any historical empire. They are not a new line. They do not introduce a new inheritance modality. The symbolic-line logic that builds them remains within the empire-R4 elaboration.

Both are platform elaborations: structures that create the conditions under which other elaborations can flourish. The reef anchors its ecosystem. The market anchors its institutional ecology. Neither breaks the line. The pattern holds across lines: bacterial mats and stromatolites at the prokaryote level, fungi at the eukaryote level, octopus and corvid cognition at the bilaterian level, religious orders and universities at the city level. Most innovation is elaboration. Lines are rare.

## Acceleration and Pacing

### Row intervals by column

| Column | Row intervals (bottom to top) |
|--------|-------------------------------|
| Genetic | ~500 MY, ~1.5 BY, ~1.2 BY (variable) |
| Neuronal | ~130 MY, ~220 MY, ~134 MY (flat, ~161 MY mean) |
| Symbolic | ~90 KY, ~5 KY, ~2 KY (accelerating) |

The genetic and neuronal lines do not accelerate within themselves. Only the symbolic line accelerates, and the mechanism is specific. Symbols mean by network position, which enables unbounded reflexivity. Each generation can take the previous generation's self-understanding as an object and operate on it, with no architectural ceiling. Genes cannot be about genes. Neurons model lower neural layers but cannot operate on the modeling-relation itself. Symbols can take their own operations as objects, indefinitely. The symbolic line accelerates because its goo removes the architectural limit on reflexive iteration that constrains both prior lines.

### Generation rates across columns

The slow loop (carrier instantiation, selection, and deprecation) runs at vastly different rates across columns. Genetic units turn over across millions of years. Symbolic-level institutional carriers turn over across decades. Same dynamic, different rates.

### Caveats

A log-linear trend fits the symbolic column's four row-emergence dates reasonably well, but the inter-stage ratios bounce between roughly 2.5× and 18×. The acceleration is real. The smoothness is not. Perreault (2012) established that rates of cultural evolution are inversely correlated with measurement interval, similar to biological rates. Some apparent acceleration in the symbolic column reflects stage-resolution. Deep history is chunked into fewer rows than recent history. The phase change at band-emergence is real. The within-line smooth acceleration is partly real and partly a chunking artifact.

### Column emergence timing

New columns consistently emerge well before the parent column would have produced its next row.

- Neuronal founding: Bilaterians emerge ~250 MY after Eumetazoa. Genetic row intervals run from ~500 MY to ~1.5 BY, so the neuronal column founds well before the genetic column would have produced a next row.
- Symbolic founding: Bands emerge ~66 MY after Primates, about 41% into the ~161 MY mean neuronal row interval.

Sample size is 2. The qualitative pattern holds across both transitions: a new column emerges well before the parent column reaches its next row. A single percentage does not summarize the pattern, because the genetic row intervals are variable rather than uniform. The pattern is suggestive but not architecturally established.

### Saturation

The symbolic line has a floor. Stage lifespan cannot compress below the cycle-times of the cycles that maintain its goo. Three versions of this claim, ordered by defensibility:

- **Strong:** stages compress until they fall below line-renewal cycles, breaking the inheritance loop. Probably false. Cycles likely rescale rather than break.
- **Moderate:** stages compress until they fall below the human developmental cycle (~20 years), at which point symbolic transmission requires bypassing biological humans. Defensible but exposed.
- **Weak (recommended):** the saturation question is the column-emergence question. When stage compression approaches biological floors, the line exits through new-column emergence rather than continuing to add rows. Consistent with the cross-column emergence pattern, and it does not require the inheritance loop to break.

## Literature Context and Contributions

### The four-paper anchor

ABHOP's cleanest position in the contemporary methodological literature is *upstream* of a cluster of four papers in personality psychology and psychopathology that converge on a single argument when read together:

- **Eronen & Bringmann (2021)** diagnoses the field's theory crisis: too few robust phenomena, weak construct validity, fat-handed interventions that move many things at once.
- **Bleidorn (2024)** applies that diagnosis to lifespan personality development and finds the field rich on phenomena, weak on causes.
- **Bareinboim et al. (2020)** supplies the formal causal-inference apparatus that distinguishes layer-1 (observational), layer-2 (interventional), and layer-3 (counterfactual) claims.
- **Ringwald (2025)** calls for *mechanistic traits*: stable person-environment dynamics measurable through intensive longitudinal designs over actuarial risk prediction.

ABHOP is not in dialogue with these four papers. It is the architectural-explanatory framework their field-level diagnoses imply the field is missing. The framework's lead move is retrodictive (Layer 1): explaining why the Big Five, Dark Triad, HiTOP, and p-factor took the structural shapes they did. Its secondary move is forward-looking but disciplined to where it has traction: predicting the structure of the mechanistic-trait taxonomy as Ringwald's program develops.

### Per-domain positioning

ABHOP's relationship to the literature differs by domain. These differences are organizing principles for the citation strategy, not material to spell out at length in any single artifact:

- **Personality and psychopathology.** Retrodictive. ABHOP explains why the field's existing taxonomies converged on the structures they did. Engages the four-paper anchor cluster directly.
- **Animal behavior and ethology.** Predictive into a gap. ABHOP supplies structural predictions where the flagship integrative framework (pace-of-life syndrome) has weak empirical support and the field has been calling for theory-driven reframing (Laskowski et al. 2022; Royauté et al. 2018).
- **Sociology and social science.** Agnostic across schools. ABHOP contributes structural commitments that any of the four major mechanism-friendly schools (analytical sociology, computational social science, causal-inference sociology, qualitative process sociology) could find compatible with their apparatus.
- **Technology studies.** Selective with explicit non-engagement of opposing positions. ABHOP's natural intellectual partners are evolutionary economics of technology and sociotechnical-transitions critique. Mainstream STS (ANT, SCOT, critical algorithm studies) is methodologically committed to treating mechanism-based explanation as the wrong vocabulary for technology, and is acknowledged briefly as the position ABHOP opposes.

### Adjacent architectural-frameworks family

ABHOP belongs to the same family as Maynard Smith & Szathmáry's *Major Evolutionary Transitions* (1995), Waring & Wood's "Evolutionary Transition in Inheritance and Individuality" (2021), and the Royal Society B special issue on socio-cultural evolution (2023). The general claim, that cultural evolution represents a new inheritance line layered on the genetic, is established. Dual inheritance theory has explained why cultural evolution is faster than genetic for decades.

What the framework adds:

- **Mechanism specificity for the symbolic phase change.** Standard explanations are demographic (more heads, more innovations) or fidelity-based (writing, printing). The argument here, that symbols mean by network position and enable unbounded reflexivity, is more architectural. Deacon's formulation of symbolic reference is the engine.
- **Parallel row structure across columns.** MET frameworks list transitions. They do not argue that genetic, neuronal, and symbolic lines have parallel internal structures (Row 1 founding, Row 2 regulation, Row 3 interiority, Row 4 differentiation). The cross-column row analysis is not in the literature.
- **Integration with Bennett's neural stack.** MET treats neural inheritance as one transition. Bennett's layered-modeling architecture gives the neuronal line its own internal structure with parallel rows. The combination is not in the literature.
- **The saturation and column-emergence prediction.** Standard cultural-evolution models predict acceleration. They do not predict the transition condition. Sample size of two column-emergence events, flagged weak.
- **Bridge-entity formalization.** Implicit in MET, but the diagram makes the parent-of-next-line pattern explicit and structural. The pattern is observed retroactively across the two confirmed line transitions. Bridge-eligibility for any given Row 4 entity is not predicted in advance.
